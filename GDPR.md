# Privacy Policy (GDPR)

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is handled when you play Platformer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Controller: Nikita Polivanchuk \
Contact: ipz231_pnr@student.ztu.edu.ua

Platformer is a locally hosted browser game built with React, TypeScript, and Vite. It does not require account registration and does not use external servers for gameplay.

## What Data We Collect
Platformer collects and stores minimal data, and only locally in your browser.

### Data stored in Local Storage:
* Username (chosen by the player for the local leaderboard)
* Game scores (for local leaderboard functionality)
* Cookie consent preference
* Age confirmation (confirmation that user is 16 years or older)

### Cookies Used:
We only use essential cookies for:
* Storing whether you accepted the cookie notice
* Storing age confirmation (16+)

### We do not use:
* Analytics cookies
* Advertising cookies
* Tracking cookies
* Third-party cookies

## Legal Basis for Processing
We process data based on:
* Consent – for cookie confirmation and age confirmation.
* Legitimate interest – to provide core gameplay functionality (e.g., storing username and local scores).

## How Your Data Is Stored
All data is stored locally in your browser using:
* Local Storage
* Cookies

Platformer does not:
* Store data on external servers
* Transmit personal data to the developer
* Share data with third parties
* Use cloud storage
* Use analytics services

Because data is stored locally, it remains on your device unless you clear your browser storage.

## Data Sharing
We do not share personal data with any third parties.

Platformer:
* Has no analytics providers
* Has no advertising networks
* Has no third-party integrations
* Has no multiplayer services

## Data Retention
Data remains stored locally in your browser until:
* You manually clear your browser storage, or
* You delete cookies, or
* You clear site data

We do not have access to or control over this data once stored on your device.

## International Data Transfers
Platformer does not transfer personal data outside your device.

No international data transfers occur.

## Children's Privacy
Platformer is intended for users aged 16 years or older.

We use a simple age confirmation mechanism stored locally in the browser.
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16.

If you believe a minor has provided personal data, please contact us.

## Data Security
Because all data is stored locally in your browser:
* No personal data is transmitted to us
* No backend database exists
* No remote storage is used

Security of local data depends on your device and browser security settings.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy if the game functionality changes.
The updated version will include a new effective date.
